Bride's Side
~ THE FIRST ROW ~

A couple months in, Paige roped Laura into a Texas road trip that turned out to be just the two of them, and that was that. They have worked together, lived downtown, and traveled all over since, from Colorado to New York. Laura recently helped move her to Amarillo. Paige serves in the Air Force.

Accompanied by

Laura was maid of honor in Ginger's wedding, and Ginger returns the favor as matron of honor (which, she will note, just means she married first). She is steady, dependable, and easy to fall right back in with. She works at Koch Industries.

Accompanied by her husband, Grant Kabler

Growing up, Laura thought Erica was the coolest person around, and as a teenager she wanted exactly that kind of self-assurance. Erica teaches earth science in Williamsburg, Brooklyn. They are both tall, and both glad about it.

Accompanied by her date, John Grabham

Laura barely knew anyone yet, and Ali jumped in right beside her. They have been close ever since, with a near-daily group chat with Ashtyn going since 2021.

Accompanied by her date, Adrian Perez

That class did more for their friendship than for their grades. Ashtyn is the friend Laura goes deepest with and the glue of her trio with Ali, loyal through anything. They played soccer together too, which still makes Laura laugh.

Accompanied by her date, Tyler Mitchell

Both Libras who can talk for hours, they read each other instantly. The hospice job sealed the friendship, including the day they learned IT reads your old messages after you leave (sorry, Jacey). Jacey is one of the funniest people Laura knows, and they are both all in on cottagecore, musicals, and anything a little grandma.

Accompanied by her date, Warren Graves

Confident and effortlessly put together, Maddie is impossible to be bored around and quick to make a friend of anyone.

Accompanied by her husband, Dylan Collette

Funny, easygoing, and fully herself, Rachel always has a pool and good company on offer.

Accompanied by her husband, Andrew Linhardt
