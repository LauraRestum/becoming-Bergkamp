A Brief History

How We Found Each Other

✦

A few short paragraphs in your voice. The day you met, the seasons in between, the porch in Colorado where the question was finally asked.

Filed by the bride

The Long Version

We met on a Tuesday in October, the kind of day where the light feels honest. He ordered a coffee. I was already at the counter, pretending not to notice him.

Three years later, on a porch in Colorado at the slow hour just before dinner, he asked. The answer had been yes for a long time.

What follows on this site is the rest, in pieces. The day. Where to stay. How to find us.

A Detail

The Lattice

The chapel carries a quiet architectural fingerprint. Diagonal iron lattice across cream limestone, repeated at every window. We have carried it into the day's stationery, into the signage, into the pages of this site.

If you look closely, you will find it again.

“A room remembers its lines.”
